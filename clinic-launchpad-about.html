Our Story

We had a clinic.
It was struggling.
Then everything changed.

We were just like you — brilliant at the work, completely lost in the business. Here's what happened next.

Where It Started

Caitlyn opened it.
Michael helped save it.

Caitlyn is an exceptional massage therapist. She opened her clinic the way most clinic owners do — with passion, skill, and almost no business knowledge. The location wasn't right. The recruiting approach was wrong. There was no financial plan.

None of that was her fault. She was never taught these things. The training that made her brilliant at her craft said nothing about leases, marketing, hiring, or cash flow.

Michael came from a business background. He looked at what Caitlyn had built and saw something worth saving — and scaling. Together, they rebuilt it from the ground up. Through trial and error, applying business experience from other industries to the clinic world.

Six years later: 4 clinic locations, 3,500 patients per month, a team of 75 — and a seven-figure exit. That hard-won knowledge is what we now give directly to you.

The Real Story

Six years.
Every stage.
Nothing held back.

This isn't a highlight reel. Every stage shaped what we teach — including the hardest decision we ever made. We chose bigger. Here's how it went.

Year 1
The Beginning
🌱

One room. Fifty patients. No roadmap.

Caitlyn had been running the clinic for a while before Michael got involved. She was talented, her patients loved her, but the business wasn't working. Wrong location. No recruitment strategy. No financial plan. The first thing they did was treat it like a real business. That changed everything.

Years 2–3
Finding the Engine
⚙️

Building the systems that made growth possible.

They rebuilt the marketing from scratch. Figured out how to recruit practitioners properly — marketing to therapists the same way they marketed to patients. Patient retention became a system. Revenue became predictable. By year three they had enough confidence to open a second location — self-funded, zero outside money.

Years 4–5
The Hardest Decision
🤔

Settle for a lifestyle business — or push for the exit?

By year four they had good income, a good team, a good life. The easy choice was to stay comfortable. The harder question: what is this actually worth? What could it be worth? They made the decision to build for an exit. They pushed. Two more locations. They documented everything. They built the kind of business a buyer would want to own.

Year 6
The Exit
🏆

Seven figures. On their terms. Clean deal.

They sold their Vancouver clinic network for seven figures. Zero outside financing throughout. The buyer wasn't just buying a business — they were buying something that worked without Michael and Caitlyn in it. That's what made it worth what it was.

What We Stand For

The values that shaped
everything we built — and teach.

These aren't words on a wall. Every one came from a real decision — often when it would have been easier to go the other way. They're why our team stayed, our patients came back, and our business was worth buying.

👥
Team First. Always.

If a patient was wronged, we made it right. If a team member was struggling, we showed up. Happy practitioners create happy patients. It's not a platitude — it's a business model.

🌿
Ethical Growth. No Shortcuts.

We built four clinics with zero outside financing. Not because we couldn't access capital — because we wanted to build something we actually owned and controlled at every step.

🤲
Practitioners Are People. Not Billings.

We empowered our team, didn't micromanage, and trusted them to do what they were trained to do brilliantly. That's why they stayed — and why they referred their friends to join us.

📍
Location Is Everything.

A mediocre clinic in the right location beats a brilliant clinic in the wrong one. We treat location decisions like investment decisions. Getting this right is one of the highest-leverage moves you'll ever make.

⭐
Above and Beyond Value.

We wanted every patient and team member to feel their experience with us was the best available. The quality of the massage, the feel of the room, the follow-up. Details aren't details — they're the brand.

🏗️
Build Something Worth Owning.

Clinic owners deserve a path that doesn't destroy their health or their love of the work. A well-built clinic gives you back your time, your income, and your life. That's not a bonus — it's the whole point.
