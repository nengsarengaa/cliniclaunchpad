Common Questions

What you're probably wondering.

What kind of clinics do you work with?

Any hands-on health clinic. If you own a clinic and you want to build it into a real business — or find your exit — we can help. The fundamentals translate across all modalities.

I'm outside Canada — can you still help?

Yes. The clinic-building framework applies globally. We work with clinic owners worldwide and adapt the systems to your local market and regulatory environment.

I'm not thinking about selling. Is this still for me?

Absolutely. Most of our work is about stepping back and scaling. Building with an exit mindset just makes you a better operator — regardless of what you decide to do in the end.

I'm burnt out. Is it too late?

It's almost never too late. Burnout is usually a systems problem, not a personal one. We'll build a realistic path forward — whether that's scaling, stepping back, or preparing a clean exit.

What makes the CL Network valuable?

Members actively refer patients to each other across cities, share staff leads, access preferred vendor pricing, and support each other's growth. It's a real business asset — not just a Facebook group.

What does the coaching engagement look like?

We'll get into specifics on your application call. Expect a structured program, direct access to Michael and Caitlyn, a custom roadmap, and real accountability — not generic advice.
